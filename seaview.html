Seaview Caravan

Seaview is a luxury 38ft x 12ft, fully serviced, with three separate bedrooms. There is one double room with en suite toilet and two twin rooms with single beds. There is a family shower room and the option of a pull out double sofa bed in the lounge.


The modern kitchen has a fridge freezer, cooker and cupboards with convenient work tops. The lounge and dining areas have modern furniture and there is also a small flat screen TV.


Double glazing and Calor Gas central heating make this new mobile home a cosy option for cooler days. Cooker and hot water are run on Calor Gas and there is an electric heater in the lounge.


The caravan will have duvets as well as pillows and blankets; but as is customary with caravans, in the interest of hygiene, our customers are asked to bring their own linen and duvet covers.

For more information, or to make a booking please call: 01841 520885 or email: sue@trevethanholidaycaravan.co.uk
