Here at Trevethan Farm we cater for families and couples looking for a quiet rural holiday. Our farm has an elevated position with panoramic sea and countryside views. What is more, it is only a five minute walk to the beautiful sandy beach of Porthcothan.

Our caravan "Seaview" is a luxury 3 bed - 6 berth caravan situated adjacent to fields with its own enclosed garden and parking alongside.

Seaview is a new 2013 model with central heating, and we maintain a no pets and no smoking policy. For more details click here.

From our position access via car to many of Cornwall's main tourist attractions is easy.

Available activities include:

Walking along the coastpaths, cycle hire on the Camel Trail, surfing at one of the many beaches and bays, sailing, wind-surfing, horse riding, fishing, The Hall for Cornwall Theatre in Truro and cinemas at both Newquay and Wadebridge.

Local restaurants include:

Jamie Oliver's Fifteen at Watergate Bay, Rick Stein's at Padstow, The Cornish Arms at St. Merryn and of course many local pubs and fish and chip shops as well.
